Introduction

Clinical expertise, shaped by story.

Dr. Elena Marie Vella works at the intersection of psychiatry, trauma recovery, research, and public education. Her approach is calm, collaborative, and grounded in the belief that recovery begins when a person feels understood before they feel examined.

About Dr. Elena Vella

EV

People rarely break in one moment.
They break slowly, in silence.

I help adults navigate the invisible burdens that affect their mental well-being. My approach is evidence-based, trauma-informed, and always tailored to the person behind the story.

Areas of Specialization

Clinical chapters, not clinic cards.

01 Trauma & PTSD

Specialist trauma-focused interventions for adults affected by complex trauma, crisis recovery, and post-traumatic stress conditions.

02 Anxiety Disorders

Assessment and treatment planning for generalized anxiety, panic disorder, stress-related symptoms, and long-term patterns of avoidance.

03 Occupational Burnout

Support for healthcare professionals, leaders, and high-pressure workers facing emotional exhaustion, occupational trauma, and resilience challenges.

04 Adult Mental Health

Psychiatric evaluation, risk assessment, crisis intervention, psychopharmacology, and multidisciplinary treatment coordination for adults aged 18 to 65.
